## Why Physical AI Matters

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI systems that operate purely in digital spaces. Instead of processing abstract data, Physical AI systems interact directly with the physical world through sensors and actuators. This enables them to understand and manipulate their environment in ways that purely digital systems cannot.

Understanding Physical AI is crucial for developing robots that can work alongside humans, navigate complex environments, and perform tasks that require physical dexterity and environmental awareness.
