# Learning Objectives - Motion Planning and Locomotion

This section outlines the specific learning objectives for the Motion Planning and Locomotion chapter.

## Primary Objectives

1. **Understand Motion Planning Fundamentals**: Comprehend the basic concepts of motion planning including configuration space, path planning, and trajectory generation.

2. **Identify Planning Algorithms**: Recognize different motion planning algorithms such as A*, RRT, and potential field methods.

3. **Analyze Locomotion Challenges**: Understand the specific challenges in humanoid locomotion including balance, stability, and terrain adaptation.

4. **Apply Planning Techniques**: Apply basic motion planning techniques to simple robotic scenarios.

5. **Evaluate Trade-offs**: Assess the trade-offs between different motion planning approaches in terms of computational complexity, completeness, and optimality.

## Secondary Objectives

1. **Configuration Space Concepts**: Understand the concept of configuration space and its role in motion planning.

2. **Control Strategies**: Recognize different control strategies for locomotion including feedback control and model predictive control.

3. **Obstacle Avoidance**: Understand techniques for dynamic obstacle avoidance during motion execution.

4. **Gait Generation**: Learn about different gait patterns and their applications in legged locomotion.
